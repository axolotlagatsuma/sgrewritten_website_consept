Stargate
Rewritten

The original, and still the best, Minecraft transportation solution!
Highly immersive, robustly capable, deeply configurable, and easily expandable!

Download

A Minecraft plugin for the Bukkit, Sponge, and PMMP ecosystems.

Cross-server

Works with Velocity, Bungee-cord or Waterfall networks.

Highly customisable

With a comprehensive and intuitive config, highly granular permissions, and multi-language support!

Immersive

Stargate's interface is integrated within gameplay; players can create, use, and destroy portals without any commands!

User-Oriented

  All of StarGate's features are designed to allow for non-admin users
to create and manage their own portal networks in a balanced and
risk free manner.

Enter Portal

Expandable

  The StarGate community has developed an ecosystem of add-ons,
collectively add integration and design-philosophy expansions to the plugin.
